My Projects

Capstone Research Project

Self-Service E-Load Kiosk

An automated mobile load dispenser kiosk — our group's most recent and most complex research project. The system integrates an ESP32 microcontroller, coin and bill acceptors, a coin hopper, and an Android tablet running a WebView-based HMI interface we designed from scratch.

The kiosk allows users to purchase prepaid mobile load credits by inserting cash, selecting a network, entering a mobile number, and receiving the load automatically — no cashier needed. The system was developed, tested, and validated following ISO/IEC 25010 software quality standards, achieving high ratings across functionality, reliability, usability, and maintainability from 30+ respondents.
