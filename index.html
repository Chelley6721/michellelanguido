What I've Built

My Projects

Capstone Research Project

Self-Service E-Load Kiosk

An automated mobile load dispenser kiosk — our group's most complex research project. The system integrates an ESP32 microcontroller, coin and bill acceptors, a coin hopper, and an Android tablet running a WebView-based HMI interface we designed from scratch.

The kiosk lets users purchase prepaid mobile load by inserting cash, selecting a network, entering a number, and receiving the load automatically — no cashier needed. Developed and validated following ISO/IEC 25010 quality standards with 30+ respondents.
